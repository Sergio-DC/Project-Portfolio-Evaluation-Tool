Depreciation Methods

Straight Line

Method used to calculate the conceded discount for the capital consumption.It is commonly used for the organizations internal planning. The recue value is substracted from the initial investment and divided by the asset's life (in years). It differs in it's depreciation calculation from the real depreciation.

MACRS (Modified accelerated cost recovery system)

Reduces taxes on the first years on an asset using a 6 category table where each category is applied to a different type of asset.
